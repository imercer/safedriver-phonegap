Overtaking

You may overtake to the right of a slow vehicle if it is safe and you have at least 100m of clear road at all times while performing the manoeuvre.
Always indicate at least 3 seconds prior to overtaking.

You can't overtake:

  • At a no-overtaking line marked by a yellow line on your side of the centreline

  • Near a pedestrian crossing, railway crossing or intersection

  • At a curve or crest of a hill where you do not have 100m of clear road during the entire manoeuvre and can't conduct the pass safely.
